Our journey in making the world a better place for seniors
2019
Emoha is born! The Emoha leadership team of experts with experience in eldercare is formed to embark on the mission of elevating the lives of seniors in their own home.
2020
During the Coronavirus pandemic, we stepped up to serve elders. With Mission Elders First, 5000+ volunteers signed up to serve 3500+ elders in NCR as goodwill.
2021
With the onset of the dreaded second wave, Emoha launched the Covid Isolation Unit (CIU) to support seniors with oxygen during this period.
2022
The Emoha app was launched– one app for all seniorcare needs. In just a few months, we achieved 100K app downloads. In 2022, our community of Emoha seniors grew to be 40,000-plus strong!
