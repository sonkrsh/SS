Why join Emoha ?

Health comes first

At Emoha, your health matters. With a beneficial family health and accidental insurance cover, we are by your side.

Flexibility at work

At Emoha, you get plenty of opportunities to work on your ideas. We trust you and your abilities and will nurture your ideas from the start to execution.

Mindfulness sessions

Not just your physical health but also your mental health matters to us. We conduct regular mindfulness sessions to keep you happy.

Learning

We empower you to give your very best. With Emoha, acquire new skills, sharpen existing ones and become better leaders. This is how we ensure the growth of not just you, but also the company.

ESOP’s

We believe in growing together and are generous with ESOPs for people who treat Emoha as their own.

Engagement

Through regular engagement activities, our employees feel a connection towards their workplace. Engaged individuals are more motivated, which in turn, increases their performance levels.
